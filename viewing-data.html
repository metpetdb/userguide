To view your own samples, you can simply select My Samples from the menu bar at the top of the page. You must be logged in to do this.

To search through your own samples with specific queries, hover over Search in the menu bar, select Samples , then click on the Provenance tab of the "Search Options" box. Under "Access," select My Samples, and then add any other queries you'd like to. (For more help with searching, see the "Searching the database" tab below.
