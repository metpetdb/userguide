To create a profile for MetPetDB, select the Login/Register button in the top right corner of any page. Fill in the text boxes in the right column under Register. You can fill in as much information as you'd like, as long as name , Email , Password , and Verify Password are all selected. Next, click Register at the bottom of the page. Wait a few moments, and then check your inbox at the email address you included. You should have an email from us with a link to complete your account's activation. Click on that link, and you will have successfully created a profile!
